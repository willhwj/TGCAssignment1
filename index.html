Why I Came Up With This Website?

COVID-19 has changed our life in many profound ways. As Singapore pivots from a strategy of ring-fencing-and-containing to co-existing with the virus, it's important that we continue living our life to the fullest, while taking all precautions to stay safe. As a lover of hotel staycations and a hospitality professional, I want to help Singapore residents to easily find hotels outside COVID-19 and dengue clusters, plan their activities and enjoy life by knowing and avoiding high-to-medium-risk areas.

This website aims to provide Singapore residents with location-specific information on COVID-19 clusters and dengue fever clusters, and hotels approved for staycation and hotels opened for Stay-Home-Notice overseas travellers.

    The main function is an interactive map that allows user to do the following things:
  • Click on a control to see all hotels in Singapore, hotels approved for staycation, hotels opened for overseas travelers who need to serve Stay-Home-Notice at dedicated facilities and staycation-approved hotels which are not opened for Stay-Home-Notice.
  • Click on a control to see COVID-19 clusters of the recent days.
  • Click on a control to see dengue clusters of the recent days.
  • Search for any location, address or building in Singapore, with the map layers selected by the user, as descrbied above.
  • Find links to official websites on COVID-19, dengue fevers, staycation-approved hotels and Stay-Home-Notice hotels.
